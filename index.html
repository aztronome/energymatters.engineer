Off-grid Solar Battery System

I started building an off-grid solar battery system to protect my family from a power outage. In the Arizona heat it is essential to have a refrigerator and air conditioner.

Astrophotography

A few years ago I dove down the rabbit hole that is astrophotography. Spending many nights in dark cold places on mouintain tops throughout the southwest I would speak sweetly to and sometimes curse my equipment and my freezing hands.

Remote Telescope Observatory

I am designing a semi-portable remote operated telescope observatory designed to be small, light and cheap while still yielding great results. Currently a 102mm carbon fiber triplet refractor operates on a celestron AVX mount that has been hypertuned. The observatory will have to protect the equipment from snow loads and wind.
