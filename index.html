Mission

I believe that through changes in our behavior and perspective we can alter our destructive course and move toward a sustainable and harmonious future. I am combining all my programming, engineering and real-world skills to make more efficient our consumption of energy around the earth. I hope to increase awareness of the amounts of energy and water each person uses in a day and the impact on our environment which future generations will inherit. My current focus is the creation of a solar energy website optimized for mobile devices.
