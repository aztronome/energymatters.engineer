Off-grid Solar

I started building an off-grid solar battery system for protection from power outages. In the Arizona heat it is essential to have a refrigerator and air conditioner.

Astrophotography

A few years ago I began astrophotography, spending many nights in cold dark places on mouintain tops throughout the southwest operating my equipment with my freezing hands trying to see backwards in time.

Observatory

I am designing a semi-portable remote operated telescope observatory designed to be small, light and cheap while still yielding great results. A 102mm carbon fiber triplet refractor on a Celestron AVX mount inhabit the prototype observatory.
