Mission

I believe that through changes in our behavior and perspective we can alter our destructive path and move toward a sustainable and harmonious future. I am combining all my programming, engineering and real-world skills to make our global energy consumption more efficient. I also hope to open many eyes to the amount of energy and water that we use each day and its impact on our enviroment so there will be some left for future generations. My current focus is on creation of a solar energy monitoring web app optimized for mobile devices.
