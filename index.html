Remote Telescope Observatory

I am designing a semi-portable remote operated telescope observatory that will eventually be placed near Heber Arizona. The system is designed to be small, light and cheap while still yielding great results. It will be controlled from the phoenix area for testing and eventually from anywhere in the world. Currently a 102mm carbon fiber triplet refractor operates on a celestron AVX mount that has been hypertuned. The observatory will have to protect the equipment from snow loads and wind.
