M31 Andromeda Galaxy

Any pictures you have seen of our own milky way galaxy are likely the Andromeda galaxy as we have no pictures taken from outside our own solar system.

M8 Lagoon Nebula

I was returning to phoneix from a tour of JPL visiting the Mars 2020 (now named Perserverence and Ingenuity) among other things. East of the CA/AZ state line I stopped near the Kofa Wildlife refuge. I struggled to get my gear set up and working before the sun came up so I only got about 12 minutes of usable data but from such a dark place that was enough to glimpse this beautiful nebula.

Mercury Transit November 11, 2019

It took me a long time to process the 153 images into this animation because the frames were significantly misaligned. I learned about scripting in photoshop to produce actions so that I could align and crop the frames. This animation is rotated from the actual transit and I was not willing to fix it. I got out the 8 inch newtonian reflector for this task as I did not have a solar filter for the refractor. Enjoy!
